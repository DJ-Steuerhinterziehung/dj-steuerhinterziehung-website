About me

Hello! I’m DJ Steuerhinterziehung (aka DJ SHZ). My real name is Georg, I live in Berlin and I produce high‑energy uptempo tracks. My speciality is a high‑energy uptempo sound that combines hard gabber beats with absurd meme samples. As a passionate raver and producer, I regularly make the crowd sweat and blend genres like hardcore and breakcore into a unique experience. Thanks to this mix I’m often called “Berlin's master of uptempo”.

My kickdrums and I have ADD – and you can hear it! My sets range between 175 and 220 BPM and are full of humour, irony and political jabs. Whether club, festival or private event: Book me or stay sad!
